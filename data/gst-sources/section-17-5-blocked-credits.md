# Section 17(5), CGST Act 2017 — Blocked Input Tax Credit

> Source: The Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (public statute). Reproduced
> for grounding TaxMind's ITC-eligibility decisions. Verify against the latest
> CBIC bare act before filing.

Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1) of section 16 and
sub-section (1) of section 18, input tax credit shall **not** be available in
respect of the following:

## Section 17(5)(a) — Motor vehicles
Motor vehicles for transportation of persons having approved seating capacity of
not more than thirteen persons (including the driver), except when they are used
for making the following taxable supplies:
- (A) further supply of such motor vehicles; or
- (B) transportation of passengers; or
- (C) imparting training on driving such motor vehicles.

## Section 17(5)(aa) — Vessels and aircraft
Vessels and aircraft except when used for the purposes specified (further supply,
transportation of passengers, training, or transportation of goods).

## Section 17(5)(ab) — Insurance, servicing, repair and maintenance
Services of general insurance, servicing, repair and maintenance in so far as
they relate to motor vehicles, vessels or aircraft referred to above, except as
specified.

## Section 17(5)(b) — Specified goods and services
Input tax credit shall not be available on the following:

### Section 17(5)(b)(i)
**Food and beverages, outdoor catering, beauty treatment, health services,
cosmetic and plastic surgery, leasing, renting or hiring of motor vehicles,
vessels or aircraft** referred to in clause (a) or (aa), **life insurance and
health insurance**.

Exception: ITC is available where an inward supply of such goods or services is
used for making an outward taxable supply of the **same category**, or as an
element of a taxable composite or mixed supply.

### Section 17(5)(b)(ii)
**Membership of a club, health and fitness centre.**

### Section 17(5)(b)(iii)
**Travel benefits extended to employees** on vacation such as leave or home
travel concession.

Proviso to clause (b): ITC in respect of the above shall be available where it is
**obligatory for an employer** to provide the same to its employees under any law
for the time being in force.

## Section 17(5)(c) — Works contract for immovable property
Works contract services when supplied for **construction of an immovable
property** (other than plant and machinery) except where it is an input service
for further supply of works contract service.

## Section 17(5)(d) — Goods/services for construction on own account
Goods or services or both received by a taxable person for **construction of an
immovable property** (other than plant or machinery) on his own account,
including when used in the course or furtherance of business.

## Section 17(5)(e) — Composition scheme
Goods or services or both on which tax has been paid under **section 10
(composition levy)**.

## Section 17(5)(f) — Non-resident taxable person
Goods or services or both received by a **non-resident taxable person** except on
goods imported by him.

## Section 17(5)(g) — Personal consumption
Goods or services or both used for **personal consumption**.

## Section 17(5)(h) — Lost, stolen, gifts, free samples
Goods **lost, stolen, destroyed, written off or disposed of by way of gift or
free samples**.

## Section 17(5)(i) — Tax under sections 74, 129, 130
Any tax paid in accordance with the provisions of sections 74, 129 and 130.
